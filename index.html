How This Works

Data. A GitHub Actions job pulls the latest World section articles from The Guardian Open Platform every 15 minutes — no manual trigger, no browser scraping.

Scoring. Headlines and standfirsts are tokenized and scored against the same NRC EmoLex + Cymbolism emotion lexicon used on my YouTube recommendations word cloud, mapping words onto Plutchik's eight core emotions.

Timeline. Every refresh is kept for 7 days. Open the Timeline above to scrub through recent history and watch the emotional makeup of the news cycle shift underneath a stacked-area chart.
